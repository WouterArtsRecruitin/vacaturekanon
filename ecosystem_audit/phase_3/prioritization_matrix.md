## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Start Small, Think Big**: Tier 1 items deliver immediate value with minimal risk
2. **Build Foundation First**: Email templates and screening enable everything else
3. **Data Before AI**: Dashboard and analytics needed before advanced automation
4. **People + Process + Technology**: Each tier increases automation sophistication
5. **ROI Accelerates**: Early wins fund larger transformations

**Total Portfolio Value: €150,000+ monthly savings within 12 months**
