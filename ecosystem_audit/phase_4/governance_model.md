## CRITICAL vs NICE-TO-HAVE

### Critical Approvals (MUST have)
1. Strategic Deal Creation
   - Why critical? High financial impact
   - Approval criteria: Detailed business case
   - Who approves? Sales Director
   - Escalation path: CEO review

2. Security-Impacting Integrations
   - Why critical? Potential data risks
   - Approval criteria: Comprehensive security audit
   - Who approves? CTO and CISO
   - Escalation path: External security consultant

### Nice-to-Have (can auto-approve)
1. Standard Role Sourcing
   - Auto-approval criteria: Predefined job description
   - Logging requirements: Full candidate details
   - Audit trail: Automated system log

2. Minor Budget Adjustments
   - Auto-approval criteria: <10% variance
   - Logging requirements: Expense category
   - Audit trail: Quarterly reconciliation
